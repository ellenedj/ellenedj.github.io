Stopmotion WarChild

Type of project

Duo photography assignment

Description

Together with my best friend we created this stop motion video for a charity. We chose WarChild and used poetry, music to tell a story of a child that has no other choice but to become a child soldier.

Since it's a stop motion video, this means that every frame you see is a picture. This project took a lot of time and effort and I'm really proud of the result.
