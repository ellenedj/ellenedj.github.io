Bird Clothes

Type of project

Photography assignment

Description

For this assignment we had to choose a bird. I wanted a unique bird from paradise. After some googling I came by this bird called "King of Saxony bird-of-paradise" (first picture). I really loved the colours and his crazy attenas makes him unique.

Next up in assignment was to dress up a model with clothes and make-up to look like the chosen bird. After I dressed up my best friend, we did a photoshoot of the pictures. These are the best pictures.

I really loved doing assignment because it was such a fun and unique way to present a bird and photograph.
