Type of project

Graduation project | Duo Project

Project goal

Influencing opinions about people with addiction, through different senses.

Tasks

Research about how our senses work and what people with addiction need. Coming up with ideas so that the outside world can experience how people with addiction feel through different senses. Designing a physical product and testing the product with people with addiction, their practitioners and family.

Description

The final product is a box with different small boxes with different items in them. There is a row of boxes for every sense. It's often hard for people with an addiction to express their feelings to the people close to them. People with an addiction can use these boxes to describe to their family or friends how they feel. For example what kind of memory do you get with the smell of coffee and how does that make you feel?
