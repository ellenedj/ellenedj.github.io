SMART Chair

Type of project

European Project Semester | Group project (4 people)

Project goal

With an international group of students, inventing a new chair that uses technology to meet the needs of the Ahrend workers.

Tasks

Research what SMART means. Interviewing Ahrend workers to so what their needs are. Design different functionalities for the chair. Assemble and design the functions on the chair. Design an app that works with the chair. Using and testing Arduino, MakeyMakey en LEDlight.

Description

With a group from different countries and from different studies we have come together to invent a new smart chair for Ahrend. I mainly focused on designing the app and helping with the MakeyMakey.The end product is a chair that has a status light to see if the chairs in the office are available, and an app to see which chairs are available and to adjust your chair for ergonomics.
