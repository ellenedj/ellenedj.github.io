Ontdekbox

Type of project

Graduation project | Duo project

Project goal

Involving children between ages 6 and 12 in the communication of Ontdekfabriek. Currently the children that visit the Ontdekfabriek don't communicate about their visit to the Ontdekfabriek so that more children visit. We want to change this.

Tasks

Researching what children that visit the Ontdekfabriek are interested in. Coming up with different ideas on how to make children give their opinion about the Ontdekfabriek. Testing different ideas with the children. Applying Gamification to the ideas.

Description

Children that visit the Ontdekfabriek love to assemble and create things. So we came up with the Ontdekbox. Ontdekbox is a box that parents can order for their children. In the box are different materials. With different themes they can make their own creation. They can share their creation with other children and so become a part of the communication. Besides creating, they can also collect points and cards that come with the box and share their creations. These points can be spent on the platform for different items.
