ABOUT ME

Welcome to my portfolio!

Who am I?

My name is Ellen de Jong. I graduated for my Bachelor's Degree in Communication and Multimedia Design, and can call myself an ''Engineer" now. Previously I graduated for my Associate degree in Graphic Design (MBO 4) and Desktop Publishing (MBO 3). In my free time I love to do small design projects for family and friends, expand my knowledge by doing tutorials, take my camera and photograph nature, do a crochet project or play games.

What is my ambition?

My ambition is to find a job where my creative ideas can be a part of solving social problems that will help society, or inform people about social problems. I am interested in working close with the people involved, research different views, getting to understand the social problem and the psychology behind it. To me it is important to work close with the people involved to design the best solution possible.

What kind of colleague am I?

I am a reserved person, but when I have an idea you will definitely hear it. I love to brainstorm as a team and listen to other people's ideas, thinking how we can put these ideas together to create a solution for a problem. After brainstorming, I like to get in my own space to do research, work out ideas and create designs. I am always up to research new topics, skills and programs.
