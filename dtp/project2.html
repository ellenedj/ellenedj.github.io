AutoWeek magazine

Type of project

Assignment

Description

When there was no assignment for me at my internship at JEVDO, they gave me the opportunity to work on my Graphic Design skills because I wanted to study Graphic Design after finishing my DTP study.

They did this by giving me the AutoWeek car magazine and told me to redesign it in my own view. To me it's a classy but sturdy looking magazine. Based on that I redesigned the logo, and used different colors and lay-outs for the magazine.

Sadly I didn't get to finish the whole magazine, only the first 2 pages. But I had a lot of fun doing so.
