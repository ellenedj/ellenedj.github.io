TEDx Let's make..

Type of project

Group project, individual assignment

Project goal

Group project: Make a product with the theme "online". The product needs to be something people can do in de breaks of the TEDx Events.

Individual assignment: Make a visual identity of a theme.

Tasks

Group project: Come up with different concepts. Design a product.

Individual assignment: Come up with a theme and design a visual identity.

Description

Group project: For the theme Let's make it online. We actually choose to make analog products. We came up with different activities that people can do in the breaks of the TEDx event. We did the complete design of one of the products. That product is an old food container, with this container you can make analog pictures. The other activities were a analog greenscreen to make a picture and an analog way to make sounds with a Makey Makey.

Individual assignment: I choose to make a visual identity for the themes "Let's make motion" and "Let's make unlimited". For "Let's make motion" I used stripes and the colours red and black as a visual identity. For "Let's make unlimited" I choose to use the universe for my visual identity.
