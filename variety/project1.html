Nighttrain experience

Type of project

Individual assignment

Project goal

Make a concept for a night train experience and apply aspects of different designers (colors, shapes, patterns) to the concept.

Tasks

Researching aspects of different designers (colors, shapes and patterns). Applying aspects to my concept in different options.

Description

The concept I came up with is a total experience room (sound,smell, seats, lights etc.) completely in a theme because people in a nighttrain can't experience what is outside. For this design I choose the theme "Dream"

The designers/projects I choose are: KossmandeJong (Hotel Bazar) from this designer I used different colors and patterns. From Studio Lernert & Sander I mostly used shapes based on the shapes they design. Lastly I applied the style of MOCKUS (UM Rechten) to the poster.
