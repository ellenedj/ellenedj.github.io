Posters

Type of project

Individual assignment | Prototyping and Designing

Project goal

Prototyping with different pictures, shapes and materials to make posters for different themes "Netherlands Circular" and "ReWire Festival".

Tasks

Making different shapes and pictures, using different pictures, shapes and materials to prototype ideas for posters. Design different posters with the inspiration from the prototypes.

Description

The end product are different posters. The first theme is about Netherlands circular. It's about litter, reusable products and pollution. Here I used different pictures I made and add quotes that can be interpreted in two different ways.

For the second series of posters I used different threads in different shapes and from different materials to get inspiration for this poster series.
